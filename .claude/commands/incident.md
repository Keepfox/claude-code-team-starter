Investigate this issue:

$ARGUMENTS

Approach:

1. Restate the observed symptom.
2. Identify likely reproduction steps.
3. Inspect the smallest relevant area first.
4. Form 2-3 plausible hypotheses.
5. Validate hypotheses with evidence from code, logs, tests, or commands.
6. Propose or implement the smallest safe fix.

Output format:

- Symptom
- Likely root cause
- Evidence
- Fix
- Verification
- Follow-up risk
